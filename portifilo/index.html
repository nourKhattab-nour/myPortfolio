About Me

Hi, I'm Nour Khattab, a senior Software Engineering student at the British University in Egypt. I love creating digital solutions that are both functional and visually appealing. With expertise in MERN stack development, UI/UX design, and a growing interest in AI integration, I aim to bridge design and technology to build meaningful applications.
